Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, or profile photos. We do not record or retain any usage data beyond what is necessary to facilitate authentication.

2. Authentication Providers

We use third-party authentication services to verify your identity:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

When you choose to log in, Google or Apple will authenticate you and provide us with a secure token to confirm your identity. We do not store your profile details or email address—only the authentication token is held temporarily in session to keep you signed in.

Note: Any personal data you share with Google or Apple remains governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential cookies or browser local storage only to maintain your logged-in session. No analytics, tracking, or advertising cookies are placed. You can clear cookies or local storage at any time via your browser settings; doing so will simply sign you out.

4. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect or store personal data, we do not share any user information with third parties.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard measures (HTTPS/TLS encryption) to protect the authentication exchange between your browser and our server. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure—if you have concerns, please contact us (see below).

6. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
